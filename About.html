Our Story

From a small corner café to a beloved community gathering place, discover the passion and people behind Aroma Coffee.

Aroma Coffee interior

A Love Letter to Coffee

Aroma Coffee began in 2010 with a simple belief: that a perfect cup of coffee can transform an ordinary moment into something extraordinary. What started as a small roasting operation in founder Maria Santos' garage has grown into a cherished neighborhood institution.

Maria's journey began on her family's coffee farm in Colombia, where she learned to appreciate every step of the coffee-making process — from cherry to cup. That reverence for craft and quality remains at the heart of everything we do.

Today, we roast all our beans in-house, partner directly with farmers who share our values, and train our baristas to be true coffee artisans. But some things never change: the warmth of our welcome, the care in every cup, and the belief that coffee is better when shared.
